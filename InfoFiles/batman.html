What is Batman Arkham Series
  • Game developed by primarily Rocksteady Studios
  • First game relased on August 25, 2009
  • Game genre is Stealth Action Adventure game
  • Batman was is originally a comicbook character created by artist Bob Kane and writer Bill Finger
  • You play as Batman a crime fighting vigilante, as he seeks to take down Escape Crimials and Psychos who are causing chaos for his city of Gotham
