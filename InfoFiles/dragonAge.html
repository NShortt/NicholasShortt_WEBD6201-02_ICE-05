What is Dragon Age
  • Game developed by BioWare
  • First relased on November 3, 2009
  • Game genre is Action RPG with an emphsis on Gameplay and Overarching Story set in a Medevial Fantansy World
  • There are three games making up the Games Triliogy
  • You play as different Customizable Characters and Classes and must journey to save the lands form Varying Threats and meet Interesting Characters to help along the way
