What is Mass Effect
  • Game developed by BioWare
  • Original game relased on November 16, 2007
  • Game genre is Action RPG/Third Person Shooter with an emphsis on Gameplay and Overarching Story
  • There are Four Games in total Three in Main Trilogy and on sequal
  • You play as a Custom character called Commander Shepard and must seek proof of an incoming Alien Threat with your ship's crew
